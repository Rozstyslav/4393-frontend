How Craft Beer Differs From Regular Beer

Craft beer is produced in limited quantities, as opposed to factory beer. Old recipes are taken as a basis and are transformed into something original, relevant, and always stylish. In the process of brewing craft beer, the main focus is on taste and ingredients. Each bottle should be perfect, and the product should not be like everyone else's.

What Main Ingredients Are There in Craft Beer?

There are four main ingredients used in beer production: water, barley malt, hops, and yeast. Water makes up 95% of beer. The water used in brewing must be of high quality. The taste of beer depends on the quality of the water. Hops give the beer its characteristic bitterness and a pleasant aroma. Hops have a unique and extremely complex chemical composition, so this ingredient cannot be replaced by any other substances without changing the quality and properties of the beer.

Which Beer to Choose for a Party With Friends?

Some people like light lager beer, while others prefer saturated imperial stout. When preparing for a party, there are two options: buy each guest's favorite beers or choose neutral styles.
